# Power-Bi--Adidas-Sales-Analysis
This project features an interactive Power BI Dashboard designed to track and analyze Adidas sales performance across the United States. It provides a high-level overview of financial health while allowing users to drill down into specific regional, temporal, and product-based trends.

# Project Overview

The analysis focuses on a multi-dimensional view of sales data (Total Sales, Profit, Units Sold, and Margin). By centralizing these metrics, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify which products are driving growth, which retailers are most effective, and how seasonality affects the bottom line.

# Business Objective

-Performance Tracking: Monitor Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) like Operating Margin ($42\%$) and Total Sales ($899.9M$).

-Market Optimization: Identify underperforming regions (e.g., South) to pivot marketing strategies.

-Inventory Management: Use sales volume trends ($2M$ units sold) to forecast future stock requirements.

-Profitability Analysis: Compare average price per unit against operating margins to ensure pricing strategies remain competitive yet profitable.

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains granular sales records including:

-Temporal Data: Transaction dates spanning from January 2020 to December 2021.

-Geographic Data: Sales broken down by Region (West, Northeast, Southeast, South, Midwest) and specific States.

-Product Categories: Men's and Women's Street, Athletic, and Apparel lines.

-Retailer Information: Sales data from major partners like West Gear, Foot Locker, Sports Direct, Kohl's, Amazon, and Walmart.

# Tools and Technologies

-Power BI Desktop: Used for data modeling, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) calculations, and visualization.

-Power Query: Utilized for data cleaning and transformation (ETL).

-Excel/CSV: The likely source format for the raw sales data.

# Key Insights Delivered

-Seasonal Trends: Sales peak significantly in July and December, suggesting a strong correlation with summer holidays and the year-end festive season.

-Top Performer: "West Gear" is the leading retailer, contributing over $\$242M$ in total sales.

-Product Dominance: Men's Street Footwear is the highest-grossing category at roughly $\$208.8M$.

-Regional Strength: The West region accounts for the largest share of sales ($30\%$), while the Midwest and South show room for growth.

-Profitability Stability: Despite fluctuations in volume, the operating margin remains healthy at $42\%$.
